# product-vision-ai
Our AI-powered service transforms a simple shop/mall photo into a comprehensive digital inventory. Here is how the process works: A picture of any shop/mall display, shelf or specific product and upload it. You receive a complete report including most products, leading company products, product details and able to count them.
